#### 2. **Data Breach Notification**
**Issue:** Notifiable Data Breaches (NDB) scheme requires specific procedures.

**Current:** Not mentioned

**Add Section:**
```
## Data Breach Notification

If we experience a data breach likely to result in serious harm, we will:
- Notify affected individuals as soon as practicable
- Notify the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC)
- Provide details of the breach, likely consequences, and steps being taken

We maintain an incident response plan in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988.
```

**Why:** Mandatory under NDB scheme since February 2018.

---

#### 3. **Automated Decision-Making Disclosure**
**Issue:** Your "AI Coach Companion" makes recommendations - need to disclose extent of automation.

**Current:** Mentioned in what you collect, but not how AI decisions are made

**Add to existing section:**
```
## How We Use Your Information

### AI-Powered Insights
Our AI Coach Companion analyzes team-level data to provide leadership recommendations. 
These insights are:
- Advisory only (not automated decisions affecting employment)
- Based on aggregated team signals, not individual metrics
- Reviewed by human coaches before critical recommendations
- Always subject to leadership judgment and context

You have the right to understand how AI recommendations are generated and to 
request human review of any AI-generated insight.
```

**Why:** APPs require transparency about automated processing, especially for workplace systems.

#### 5. **Clarify De-Anonymization Risk**
**Current:** States team-level data only, but doesn't address small team risk

**Add:**
```
## Privacy Protection for Small Teams

For teams with fewer than 5 members, aggregated data may be reasonably 
identifiable to individuals. In these cases:
- We apply additional privacy safeguards
- Leaders receive summary insights only (no granular breakdowns)
- Sensitive signals are suppressed if identifiability risk is high

We will notify you if your team size creates privacy considerations.
```

**Why:** Shows you've thought about edge cases; important for workplace privacy.

#### 6. **Workplace Privacy Considerations**
**Current:** Focuses on consumer privacy, but this is a B2B workplace tool

**Add New Section:**
```
## Workplace Privacy Notice

CultureCrunch is designed for workplace use. If you are an employee whose 
organisation uses CultureCrunch:

- **Data Collection:** Your team leader may access team-level insights that 
  include signals from your participation
- **Individual Privacy:** We do NOT share individual responses, scores, or 
  behavioral data with your employer
- **Voluntary Participation:** Consult your organisation's policies regarding 
  participation expectations
- **Complaints:** You may raise concerns with us at privacy@culturecrunch.io 
  or your organisation's privacy officer

Organisational customers are required to notify employees about CultureCrunch 
use in accordance with APP 5 (notification of collection).
```

**Why:** Fair Work Act and APPs have specific requirements for employee monitoring/data collection.
