## Risk Mitigation

### Accuracy Concerns
**Mitigation:**
- Display confidence intervals (conservative/typical/optimistic)
- Transparent assumptions and limitations section
- Link to full methodology with citations
- Disclaimer: "Projections based on research averages; results vary"

### Skepticism About Numbers
**Mitigation:**
- Show research sources prominently
- Provide case studies and validation
- Offer consultation to discuss specific situation
- Emphasize: "These are YOUR numbers, not ours"

### Comparison Shopping
**Mitigation:**
- Differentiate operating system model from event-based training
- Show why traditional training fails (supported by research)
- Emphasize implementation quality over content
- Offer pilot program to prove value
